Quy Tran - Register Nurse

Quy values excellent patient care, research, and continuous education. In his nursing care, he strives to transform patient health and outcomes through high quality individualized care, effectively collaboration with a multidisciplinary team, and personal responsibility to care for the patients and their family members.

Graduate with Outstanding Award in Bachelor Degree in Nursing in 20015, Quy is an individual who always works hard in both of his professional and personal life. He has almost ten years of clinical experience as a Registered Nurse, cultivated from various comprehensive and challenging patient care environments on Critical Care/Intensive care units at UC Irvine.

Quy is a great nurse who always looks out for his coworker and swoop in to help without hesitation when they are in need.
